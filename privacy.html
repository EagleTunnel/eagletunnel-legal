Privacy Policy for Eagle Tunnel

Effective date: June 29, 2026

Eagle Tunnel is a VPN tunnel management app for Android. This privacy policy explains what information the app accesses, uses, and shares.

Information We Collect

Eagle Tunnel does not collect, sell, or share personal information with the developer.

The app stores VPN profiles and configuration data locally on your device. These profiles may include server addresses, public keys, and other VPN connection settings that you import or create.

Permissions and Local Access

Eagle Tunnel may request access to the following Android features:

  • Camera: used only to scan QR codes for importing VPN profiles.
  • Files selected by you: used only to import VPN profile files or QR code images.
  • VPN and network access: used to create, manage, and monitor VPN tunnel connections.
  • Boot completed: used to restore tunnel state when that behavior is enabled.
  • System overlay or quick settings features: used only where required by Android for VPN-related controls.

How VPN Traffic Works

When you enable a VPN tunnel, your network traffic is routed according to the VPN profile you selected. The developer of Eagle Tunnel does not inspect, collect, or store your browsing activity, DNS queries, or network traffic.

Data Storage

VPN profiles and settings are stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, this local data may be removed by Android. If you export profiles, the exported files are saved only where you choose.

Data Sharing

Eagle Tunnel does not sell user data. Eagle Tunnel does not share user data with advertisers or analytics providers.

Third-Party Services

Eagle Tunnel does not use advertising SDKs or analytics SDKs.

Data Security

Eagle Tunnel uses Android system APIs and VPN functionality to manage tunnels. You are responsible for importing VPN profiles only from providers or servers you trust.

Children's Privacy

Eagle Tunnel is not directed to children. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact the developer using the support email listed for Eagle Tunnel on Google Play.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted at this same URL.
